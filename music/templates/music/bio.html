Bio

DJ McLovin is Jim Mortko. He's a avid fan of many genres of music and been DJing for over 30 years. He has a true passion for discovering new tunes, blending smooth mixes and making people happy.

When it comes it comes to DJing there are essentially 3 important principals every DJ should have strengths in:
  • A broad understanding and continuing exploration in various types of music - and a music collection to back it up.
  • The skills to work with your equipment, technology and abilities to beat-match songs.
  • The ability to read the crowd and connect wiht people, so you're consistently making the right choices in what you play.
