Bio

DJ McLovin is Jim Mortko.

He's an avid fan of many genres of music and been DJing for over 30 years. He has a true passion for discovering new tunes, blending smooth mixes and making people happy.

When it comes it comes to DJing there are essentially 3 important principals every DJ should have strengths in:
  • A broad familiarity and continuing exploration of various types of music - and the music collection to back it up.
  • The skills to work with your equipment, technology and abilities to make it all work together seamaessly.
  • The ability to read the crowd and really connect with people, so you're consistently making the right choices in what you play.
