Bio

DJ McLovin is Jim Mortko.

I'm an avid fan of many genres of music and have been DJing for over 30 years.

I have a true passion for discovering new tunes, blending smooth mixes and making people happy.

Things I think are important:

  • A broad familiarity and continuing exploration of various types of music - and the music collection to back it up.
  • The skills to work with your equipment / tech and to make it all come together when you need it to.
  • The ability to read the crowd and really connect with people.
